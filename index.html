How to Play Prime Poker

Prime Poker is a mathematical blackjack variant where composites can be fractured into primes. Outscore the dealer without exceeding the Target.

Special Actions

  • Split: Composite cards (e.g. 15) can be "fractured" into their prime factors (e.g. 3 and 5). Primes cannot be split! This helps you lower your total safely.
  • Discard: Once per hand, you can toss away a card to lower your total. Strategic discard can save a busting hand.

Game Modes

♠ Classic Mode

  • Deck: Ace (1) to King (13)
  • Target: 21
  • Dealer stands at: 17
  • Goldbach: Two primes = 21

★ Prime Mode

  • Deck: 2 through 19
  • Target: 42
  • Dealer stands at: 35
  • Goldbach: Two primes = 42

Payout Tiers

  • Goldbach Natural (3x): Exactly two prime cards summing to the Target.
  • Prime Target (2.5x): Hitting the Target using only prime cards.
  • Factored Target (2x): Hitting the Target using at least one split composite.
  • Ordinary Target (1.5x): Hitting the Target exactly.
